This is the description to the source model of pulsar -- Crab Pulsar
3 pulsars with different flux level are included in DC2-Galactic-Pulsar

Target: PSR J0534+2200 (Crab pulsar)
Pulse profile: Kuiper et al. 2001, A&A 378, 918		https://doi.org/10.1051/0004-6361:20011256
Spectrum(Pulsar+Nebula): Petry et al. 2009, A&A 507, 549		https://doi.org/10.1051/0004-6361/200912844 
The source is divided into 5 energy bands.
Band1 100-315 keV
Band2 315-750 keV
Band3 0.75-1 MeV
Band4 1-3 MeV
Band5 3-10 MeV

Goal: Detection, Phase-integrated spectrum, Phase-resolved spectrum(DC3 or later), Pulse profile(DC3 or later)
